About Research Teaching

I am a PhD student at the University of Birmingham in the Theory of Computation research group, working under the supervision of Vincent Rahli and Martín Escardó.

Before joining Birmingham as a PhD researcher, I completed my Master's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science at Imperial College London. My Master's thesis looked at Allen's interval algebra from the point of view of model theory and was supervised by Robert Barham.
